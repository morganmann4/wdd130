FAQ'S

Popular Questions


What is the return policy?

To return a product, fill out the form on our Support Page with your order number, place of purchase, and other relevant information. After submission, we will contact you to begin the return process.

When will my order arrive?

Standard Shipping: allow 7-10 business days for delivery. Expedited Shipping: allow 2-5 business days for delivery. Overnight Shipping: allow 1-2 business days for delivery.

Can I cancel my order?

Orders cannot be cancelled. You can request a return by contacting us with your order number. You may cancel your order for our other products within one hour of ordering by submitting a form with your order number. The refund can take 1-3 business days depending on your bank.

How do I track my order?

When you make a purchase an email comformation along with a tracking number is sent to the email your account is under.

Do you ship internationally?

We currently only ship to states in the U.S. excluding Hawii and Alaska
