FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions from our customers.

What cake size would be appropriate for my occasion?

We offer total 4 different size options, here are the general portions each size offers:

Small: about 10~12 poritions
Medium: 13~25 portions
Large: 40~55 portions
Extra Large: 100 portions

We suggest having small~medium for relatively smaller birthday parties, baby showers, and suggest having anything beyond Large option for bigger occassions like weddings and parties.

Is it possible to pick up my cake from the store?

Dreamy Cake offers delivery options for our clients, which safely gets your beautifully customized cakes to your location. For this reason, we strongly suggest that our customres use the delivery option to avoid any damages done to the cake.

In order to pick-up your own cake, choose "pick-up" option on the delivery option after customizing your cake. Carefully read the liability agreement terms, and click "I Agree." Then, choose the closest location and choose a pick-up time.Your cake will be ready to be picked up on the promised time.

When should I order my cake so it can be delivered on time?

We value customer satisfaction, and therefore we always strive to deliever the best quality product to our clients.

In order for us to do this, we take orders a week in advance at minimum. However, we strongly recommend to order two weeks in advance.
