Site Name

The Record Lounge

Site Purporse

An online record store where people can browse through the stores selection of record, discover new music and purchase records.

Target Audience

Vintage records appeal to a wide variety of people. But with the store being an online shop, it will limit the amount of older guests. This store will be designed to target the younger audience. People can find the stores page on any device that has internet.

Content

Home

This is the main page. When customers first visit the website this will be the page they are taken to. It will be an attention grabber. On this page it will display the company name and logo along with attention catching details such as new record releases, deals on records, pictures of the stores most popular records or records for the season.

Explore

On this page customers will be able to browse through the stores records. All of these records will have a price and be available for purchase. There will be ways to browse through specific music genres or search for records based on an artist name or an alblum name.

About Us

On this page it will talk about the stores purpose, the benefits of this store being an online shop for records, and history behind records. The stores purpose is for it to be a place where people can look through the stores selection of records, discover new music, purchase records and be informative of new records being released. The benefits of an online shop are that it is conveinient because customers can shop from the comfort of their own home, records are all in one place, and they can easily find a record they may be looking for by using a search or filter method on our explore page. Also with the covid pandemic it allows people to isolate but still have access to this hobby they enjoy. Some history behind vinyl records is that they were most popular during the 1950's until 1980's when they began being replaced by cds. They modern vinyl record was first introduced in 1948.
